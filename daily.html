Moon Phase: November 21, 2024

On November 21st the Moon's phase is a waning crescent. When the moon is waning it means the moon is moving from a full moon to a half moon so it gives the appearence that the illumination is decreasing day by day. The moon is still in its early waning phases since its illumination is only 12%. The moon age corresponds to the days since the last new moon.
